FAQs




Students of any education level from anywhere in the world are eligible to apply for CoffeHacks!

Admission to CoffeeHacks is completely free! We will provide all the resources and mentorship our hackers need to build something incredible.

You can form teams of up to 4 people. There are no restrictions for who they can be, so you can team up with hackers from any school, country, or experience level. Teams can be formed before or during the event.

CoffeeHacks will take place on February 11th - 13th, 2022.

All projects must be submitted on Devpost. Participants must submit their projects before the deadline to be eligible for juding.

Please email to coffeehacks@gmail.com.
